This assessment focused on identifying and validating security weaknesses in a controlled laboratory environment to simulate real-world cyber threats. The process involved scanning systems for vulnerabilities, analyzing publicly available information, and attempting safe exploitation to understand the potential risks. Findings included outdated software versions, exposed network services, weak authentication mechanisms, and web application flaws that could allow attackers to gain unauthorized access. The report recommends timely patching, secure configuration, service hardening, and improved monitoring to reduce exposure.
